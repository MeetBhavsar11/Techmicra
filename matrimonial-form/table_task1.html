MB Informatics Pvt. Ltd.

MB Informatics Pvt. Ltd. (MBI) is a process management and consulting company that started its journey in 2011 by offering support to companies on Finance & Accounting (F&A) processes. Over the last five years, it has expanded its portfolio to cover other services such as Payroll, HR, Recruitment, Process Consulting, Legal and Portal Management. The company serves 15+ business entities and currently provides services to companies in 10+ countries around the globe.
